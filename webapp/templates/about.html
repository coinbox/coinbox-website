About Us

This project was started by Miguel Chavez Gamboa, Jad Kik, and Benjamin Burt.

  • Miguel Chavez Gamboa is the founder of LemonPOS (May 2007) a linux based C++/Qt/KDE POS program.
  • Jad Kik is the founder of wxpos (August 2011) a cross platform python/wx POS program.
  • Benjamin Burt has help with documentation and testing of LemonPOS.

This project is designed to be FLOSS project, that is available for others to use and enjoy. We hope to build this project to be helpful and easy to understand. We understand that it is important to know how your business is running, and to do that you need to have control of products and prices. This program is designed with this in mind, and also the ease of usability so that someone with limited computer knowledge could make good use of it.

About the Name and Logo

The name Coin Box POS came about after much conversation about what the name should be. Where we were merging two different projects we wanted to give it a new name. Our main goal we wanted to accomplish with the name is make it easy to know what the software tool is, and to make it friendly. The image of a coin box spilling out its coins is to symbolize two things. The first is that this software tool is open, it is our intent to keep it "free" like "freedom" or "free speech". "Freedom" in the ability to change and modify the software to meet your needs, and "free" in cost. The second is that we intend to create a tool that will help you grow your business out of your "box".
